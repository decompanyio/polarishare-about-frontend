The POLARIS SHARE project is established as the DApp on the blockchain platform, which can generate tokens that form its economy.
Because important data and transaction records are stored in blockchain, it is implemented with the reliable distributed transaction method that it is not easy to manipulate.
However, the service will be provided as the hybrid way at the initial stage, the way which is mixed of blockchain and current cloud technologies due to the performance and cost problems of the blockchain platform.
