Document Sharing and Selling
Knowledge producers can directly upload various types of documents, such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint and PDF, created with a variety of document creation software such as the Polaris Office. Documents containing knowledge on particular subjects as well as various documents such as Excel files containing various formats and formulas to solve any problem are available for uploading. Documents may be shared for free or at the prices previously designated by knowledge producers.
Document Finding
Documents uploaded to the POLARIS SHARE are forwarded to the Users through various channels. In major routes, there are the methods of exposure to main Web pages, movement as per category, search within sites and related contents, external search, reference links, and embedding to other sites.

On the main webpage, you can not only show a specific knowledge but also search for the latest popular documents, or you are available to select specific categories and search for required documents.

Documents uploaded to the POLARIS SHARE are optimized for search engines and can easily be exposed to major search sites such as Google and Yahoo. Once documents covering various topics are uploaded to the POLARIS SHARE, they can be searched by major search engines and thus it attracts more users.
Document Reading and Vote
The users of POLARIS SHARE can evaluate the document by reading the shared document, leaving comments or voting for.

These results are used to increase the possibility of exposure of content within the site by influencing the screening of documents to be exposed to the main web page, the sort order of search results, and the probability of exposure in related content.
Rewards for knowledge Producers
Knowledge producers and other contributors are rewarded according to their degree of contribution respectively.
