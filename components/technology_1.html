POLARIS SHARE and Blockchain
Although the project is provided as a hybrid type, it will be designed in the ways that decentralization, independence and reliability Â are guaranteed, and the most parts of the project will be implemented on the blockchain as the blockchain technology will gradually improve more and more in the future. The choice of items to be stored in the blockchain will be determined by the data volume, the required processing speed and the requested level of reliability.

For example, the data related to documents can be divided into the document itself, the meta-data of documents, and the activity data of documents such as the numbers of comments and views. The document itself is difficult to record on the current blockchain due to its large size, but verification of whether the document is original becomes an important issue later, considering document copyright issues. Thus, the system is constructed in reliable ways that allows the creation time and identification of the originals by that data itself is stored in off-chains and hash values are stored in on-chains.

Also, although built through the cloud, collaborative tools, including Polaris Office as well as various Office software, will be connected and used through open APIs. The frontend is implemented through this and POLARIS SHARE team can look over data at the same level.

Architecture Extension
Although the POLARIS SHARE project currently provides only knowledge transaction services as proposed services, it aims to become the platform that provides expert network oriented and distributed work environments beyond the knowledge transaction

Initially, we have a plan to expand to collaborative services such as profile, human network services, project management services, business messengers and video chat, starting from knowledge exchanges and a partner App, Office software.

The Open API of The POLARIS SHARE will enable projects to expand service platforms by providing API, thereby these services can be executed on the common basis which is stored in blockchain.
POLS in the POLARIS SHARE
POLS is a commodity for using various knowledge transaction services.
The standard tokens of ERC20 specification can be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges by adding specific functions,
and on the POLARIS SHARE platform, it can be used for the collections, the rewards, the exchanges, or the other purposes of user and document contents.
